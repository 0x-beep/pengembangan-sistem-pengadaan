## INTRODUCTION & OVERVIEW

### Who Can Apply?

PT KMU is seeking qualified vendors to supply:
- **Alkes** (Medical Equipment & Devices)
- **Farmasi** (Pharmacy & Medicines)
- **Bahan Habis Pakai** (General Consumables)
- **Bahan Medis Habis Pakai** (Medical Consumables)
- **Jasa** (Services: Rentals, Construction, Renovation, Maintenance)
- **Reagents & Lab Supplies**

### Regional Focus: Kalimantan Timur

PT KMU operates across Kalimantan Timur. We prioritize vendors who can serve:

```
Primary Cities (Utama):
├─ Balikpapan (Headquarters - Primary focus)
├─ Bontang (Major operations)
├─ Samarinda (Regional hub)
└─ Kutai Timur (Remote area - Special requirements)

Secondary Areas (Sekunder):
└─ Bengalon (Frontier location)

Vendor Preference:
✅ LOCAL vendors (based in Kaltim)    → PREFERRED
✅ REGIONAL vendors (Java but can serve Kaltim)    → ACCEPTABLE
⚠️ JAKARTA-based vendors (must have local reps)    → WITH CONDITIONS
```

### Registration Process Overview

```
COMPLETE PROCESS: 4-6 WEEKS

Week 1:  Initial Application
         └─ Submit forms + basic documents

Week 2:  Document Review
         └─ PT KMU reviews completeness

Week 3:  Qualification Assessment
         └─ Financial, legal, operational check

Week 4:  Site Visit (if required)
         └─ Inspect facilities or references

Week 5:  Approval or Request for Changes
         └─ Decision made

Week 6:  Contract Signing & Activation
         └─ Become registered vendor

Result:  Vendor ID assigned (VND-XXX)
         KSO contract created
         Vendor portal account activated
```

## LEGAL DOCUMENTS

### Required Legal Documents (Dokumen Legal Yang Diperlukan)

**All documents must be:**
- Original or certified copies
- In good condition (legible)
- Not expired
- In Indonesian or English (with certified translation if needed)

### Checklist 1: Business Registration Documents

```
☐ 1. BUSINESS LICENSE (SIU - Surat Izin Usaha)
    └─ Status: Currently valid (not expired)
    └─ Issued by: Local government (Bupati/Walikota)
    └─ Required copies: 1 original + 2 copies
    └─ Valid period: Minimum 1 year from application date
    └─ For Kaltim vendors: Must be issued by local Kaltim authority
    
☐ 2. COMPANY REGISTRATION (BPJS - Badan Pendaftaran Wajib Pajak)
    └─ Status: Active
    └─ Certificate of Company Registration (Akta Pendirian)
    └─ Company deed number & date
    └─ Required copies: 1 original + 2 copies
    
☐ 3. TAXPAYER IDENTIFICATION NUMBER (NPWP - Nomor Pokok Wajib Pajak)
    └─ Company NPWP (not individual)
    └─ Issue date & expiry (if applicable)
    └─ Required copies: 1 original + 2 copies
    └─ Must match company name exactly
    
☐ 4. VALUE ADDED TAX REGISTRATION (PKP - Pengusaha Kena Pajak)
    └─ Status: Active (if applicable to vendor type)
    └─ Registration certificate
    └─ Required copies: 1 original + 2 copies
    
☐ 5. EXPORT/IMPORT REGISTRATION (if applicable)
    └─ HS Code clearance
    └─ Customs registration
    └─ Only required if: Vendor imports goods
    └─ Required copies: 2 copies
    
☐ 6. COMPANY DOMICILE LETTER (Surat Domisili Perusahaan)
    └─ Issued by: Sub-district head (Lurah/Camat)
    └─ Confirming: Company is based at stated address
    └─ Max age: 6 months old
    └─ Required copies: 1 original
    
☐ 7. NOTARIZED COMPANY PROFILE
    └─ Contains: Business nature, capital, shareholders, management
    └─ Issued by: Notary (Notaris)
    └─ Max age: 1 year
    └─ Required copies: 1 certified + 1 copy
```

### Checklist 2: Management & Ownership Documents

✅ Lower thresholds acceptable if:
   ├─ Strong local presence

   ├─ Can serve all 5 cities/areas
